## 1. 前情回顾

### 1.1 概率流ODE

1. 前向与反向扩散过程都可以用连续域中的SDE描述。
2. 扩散过程的SDE存在一个对应的ODE，其在每个时间步具有相同的边际分布。
3. 因此，反向扩散（生成）过程可以通过求解对应的ODE来完成。

### 1.2 Fokker-Planck方程

$$
dx_t = f(x_t, t)dt + g(x_t, t)dw
$$

$$
\implies \frac{\partial p_t(x)}{\partial t} = -\nabla_{x_t} \cdot [f(x_t, t)p_t(x_t)] + \frac{1}{2}\nabla_{x_t}^2 [g(x_t, t)g(x_t, t)^T p_t(x_t)]
$$

### 1.3 DPM-Solver

为扩散模型ODE设计专用求解器：

$$
\frac{d x(t)}{d t} = \left(\frac{d \log \alpha_{t}}{d t}\right) x(t) - \sigma_{t} \frac{d \lambda_{t}}{d t} \hat{\epsilon}_{\theta}(x_{t}, t)
$$

## 2. 今日主题：基于流的模型

- 一类生成模型，通过一系列可逆变换将基础分布转换为复杂目标分布。
- 变换通常用ODE参数化。
- 流模型在深度生成模型中研究早于扩散模型。

**代表性工作：**

- Rezende & Mohamed, Variational Inference with Normalizing Flows, ICML 2015.
- Dinh et al., NICE, ICLR 2015.
- Dinh et al., Real NVP, ICLR 2017.
- Grathwohl et al., FFJORD, ICLR 2019.
- Ho et al., Flow++, ICML 2019.
- Chen et al., Residual Flows, NeurIPS 2019.
- Behrmann et al., Invertible Residual Networks, ICML 2019.
- Lipman et al., Flow Matching for Generative Modeling, ICLR 2023.

## 4. Normalizing Flow

### 4.1 定义

设$\phi$为连续可微函数，其逆$\phi^{-1}$也存在且连续可微。

-$p_0(x_0)$：参考分布
-$p_1(x_1)$：由以下采样过程诱导的分布

$$
\begin{align*}
x_0 &\sim p_0 \\
x_1 &= \phi(x_0)
\end{align*}
$$

### 4.2 变量变换

概率质量守恒：

$$
p_{1}(x_{1})dx_{1} = p_{0}(x_{0})dx_{0}
$$

给定$x_0 = \phi^{-1}(x_1)$，雅可比行列式：

$$
dx_{0} = \left|\operatorname{det}\left(\frac{\partial\phi^{-1}(x_{1})}{\partial x_{1}}\right)\right|dx_{1}
$$

因此：

$$
p_{1}(x_{1}) = p_{0}(\phi^{-1}(x_{1})) \left|\operatorname{det}\left(\frac{\partial\phi^{-1}(x_{1})}{\partial x_{1}}\right)\right|
$$

$$
\log p_{1}(x_{1}) = \log p_{0}(\phi^{-1}(x_{1})) + \log \left|\operatorname{det}\left(\frac{\partial\phi^{-1}(x_{1})}{\partial x_{1}}\right)\right|
$$

记 push-forward 算子为$[\phi]_{*}$：

$$
p_{1}(x_{1}) = [\phi]_{*}p_{0}(x_{1})
$$

### 4.3 小测验

设：

$$
p_0(x_0) = \mathcal{N}(\mu, \sigma^2) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}} e^{-\frac{(x_0-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
$$
$$
\phi(x_0) = ax_0 + b \quad (a \neq 0)
$$
$$
\phi^{-1}(x_1) = \frac{x_1 - b}{a}
$$

**问：**$p_1(x_1) = [\phi]_{*}p_0(x_1)$是什么？

**答案：**

$$
\begin{align*}
[\phi]_{*}p_0(x_1) &= p_0 \left( \frac{x_1 - b}{a} \right) \frac{1}{a} \\
&= \frac{1}{a} \cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}} e^{-\frac{\left(\frac{x_1 - b}{a} - \mu\right)^2}{2\sigma^2}} \\
&= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi(a\sigma)^2}} e^{-\frac{(x_1 - (a\mu + b))^2}{2(a\sigma)^2}} \\
&= \mathcal{N}(a\mu + b, a^2\sigma^2)
\end{align*}
$$

### 4.4 学习目标

给定来自未知数据分布$p_1$的样本$x_1$，学习可逆函数$\phi_{\theta}(x_0)$：

$$
\mathcal{L} = -\mathbb{E}_{x_1\sim p_1}\log ([\phi_\theta ]_*p_0(x_1))
$$

### 4.5 挑战

- 如何学习可逆函数并计算逆？
- 如何计算雅可比行列式？

## 5. Residual Flow [Chen et al. 2019]

将$\phi$表示为多个函数的复合：

$$
\phi = \phi_{(T - 1)\cdot \Delta t}\circ \dots \circ \phi_{1\cdot \Delta t}\circ \phi_{0\cdot \Delta t}
$$

其中：

$$
\phi_{t}(x_{t}) = x_{t} + \Delta t\cdot u_{t}(x_{t}), \quad \Delta t = \frac{1}{T}
$$

---

## 6. Continuous Normalizing Flow

当$T \to \infty \Rightarrow \Delta t \to 0$：

$$
\lim_{\Delta t\to 0}\frac{\phi_t(x_t) - x_t}{\Delta t} = \lim_{\Delta t\to 0}\frac{x_{t + \Delta t} - x_t}{\Delta t} = \frac{d x_t}{d t} = u_t(x_t)
$$

ODE 形式：

$$
x_{t} = x_{0} + \int_{0}^{t}u_{s}(x_{s})ds = x_{0} + \int_{0}^{t}u_{s}(\phi_{s}(x_{0}))ds
$$

### 6.1 小测验

回忆 Fokker-Planck 方程：

$$
dx_t = f(x_t, t)dt + g(x_t, t)dw
$$
$$
\Rightarrow \frac{\partial p_t(x)}{\partial t} = -\nabla \cdot [f(x_t, t)p_t(x_t)] + \frac{1}{2}\nabla^2[g(x_t, t)g(x_t, t)^Tp_t(x_t)]
$$

**问：** 当$\frac{dx_t}{dt} = u_t(x_t)$时，$\frac{\partial p_t(x_t)}{\partial t}$是什么？

**答案：**

$$
\frac{dx_t}{dt} = u_t(x_t) \Rightarrow \frac{\partial p_t(x_t)}{\partial t} = -\nabla \cdot [u_t(x_t)p_t(x_t)]
$$

### 6.2 概率密度演化

$$
\frac{\partial p_t(x_t)}{\partial t} = -\nabla \cdot [u_t(x_t)p_t(x_t)]
$$
$$
\implies \frac{\partial \log p_t(x_t)}{\partial t} = -\nabla \cdot u_t(x_t)
$$
$$
\implies \log p_1(x_1) = \log p_0(x_0) - \int_0^1 \nabla \cdot u_t(x_t) dt
$$

### 6.3 学习目标

学习向量场$u_\theta(x_t, t)$：

$$
\mathcal{L} = -\mathbb{E}_{x_1\sim p_1}\log p_1(x_1)
$$

### 6.4 挑战

- 计算目标函数需要求解ODE，非常耗时。

## 7. Conditional Flow Matching [Lipman et al., ICLR 2023]

### 7.1 Flow Matching 目标

假设已知向量场$u_t(x_t)$满足：

$$
\log p_1(x_1) = \log p_0(x_0) - \int_0^1 \nabla \cdot u_t(x_t) dt
$$

这样的向量场是否唯一？不唯一。

学习该向量场：

$$
\mathcal{L} = \mathbb{E}_{t\sim \mathcal{U}[0,1]}\mathbb{E}_{x_t\sim p_t}[\| v_\theta(x_t,t) - u_t(x_t)\|^2]
$$

与扩散模型中的噪声预测网络类似：

$$
\mathbb{E}_{x_0 \sim q(x_0), t > 1, q(x_t | x_0)} [ \| \hat{\varepsilon}_\theta(x_t, t) - \varepsilon_t \|^2 ]
$$

### 7.2 问题

我们不知道$u_t(x_t)$，如何最小化目标？

### 7.3 条件概率路径

定义条件概率分布$p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1)$：

$$
p_{t}(x_{t}) = \int p_{t|1}(x_{t}|x_{1})p_{1}(x_{1})dx_{1}
$$
$$
p_{0|1}(x_0|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(0, I)
$$
$$
p_{1|1}(x_1|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(x_1,\sigma_{\min}^2 I)
$$

其中$\sigma_{\min}$是一个很小的值。

### 7.4 条件概率路径的选择

考虑一族正态分布：

$$
p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(\mu_t(x_1), \sigma_t^2(x_1)I)
$$

满足：

$$
\mu_0(x_1) = 0,\ \sigma_0(x_1) = 1
$$
$$
\mu_1(x_1) = x_1,\ \sigma_1(x_1) = \sigma_{\min}
$$

### 7.5 条件向量场

从 Fokker-Planck 方程：

$$
\frac{\partial p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1)}{\partial t} = -\nabla \cdot [u_t(x_t|x_1)p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1)]
$$

条件向量场不唯一。

一种简单的选择：

$$
\phi_t(x_0|x_1) = \sigma_t(x_1)x_0 + \mu_t(x_1)
$$
$$
u_t(x_t|x_1) = \frac{\sigma_t'(x_1)}{\sigma_t(x_1)}(x_t - \mu_t(x_1)) + \mu_t'(x_1)
$$

### 7.6 重要事实1：边际向量场

$$
u_t(x_t) = \int u_t(x_t|x_1)\frac{p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1)p_1(x_1)}{p_t(x_t)} dx_1
$$

**证明思路：** 利用条件概率路径的演化方程和边际分布的定义。

### 7.7 重要事实2：损失函数的等价形式

$$
\mathcal{L} = \mathbb{E}_{t\sim \mathcal{U}[0,1]}\mathbb{E}_{x_t\sim p_t}[\| v_\theta(x_t,t) - u_t(x_t)\|^2]
$$

等价于：

$$
\mathcal{L} = \mathbb{E}_{t\sim \mathcal{U}[0,1]}\mathbb{E}_{x_1\sim p_1, x_t\sim p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1)}[\| v_\theta(x_t,t) - u_t(x_t|x_1)\|^2]
$$

### 7.8 采样$x_t \sim p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1)$

利用条件流映射：

$$
x_0 \sim p_0
$$
$$
x_t = \phi_t(x_0|x_1) = \sigma_t(x_1)x_0 + \mu_t(x_1)
$$

因此损失函数可写为：

$$
\mathcal{L} = \mathbb{E}_{t \sim \mathcal{U}[0,1]} \mathbb{E}_{x_1 \sim p_1, x_0 \sim p_0} [ \| v_\theta(\phi_t(x_0|x_1), t) - u_t(\phi_t(x_0|x_1)|x_1) \|^2]
$$

## 8. 条件概率路径的具体例子

### 8.1 VP-SDE（DDPM）与 VE-SDE（SMLD）

- **VP（方差保持）**：

$$
p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(\alpha_{1-t}x_1, (1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2)I)
$$

- **VE（方差爆炸）**：

$$
p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(x_1, \sigma_{1-t}^2 I)
$$

### 8.2 小测验1：VP情况下的条件向量场

已知：

$$
p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(\alpha_{1-t}x_1, (1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2)I)
$$

**问：**$u_t(x_t|x_1)$是什么？

**答案：**

$$
\mu_t(x_1) = \alpha_{1-t}x_1 \Rightarrow \frac{d}{dt}\mu_t(x_1) = -\alpha_{1-t}'x_1
$$
$$
\sigma_t(x_1) = \sqrt{1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2} \Rightarrow \frac{d}{dt} \sigma_t(x_1) = \frac{\alpha_{1-t}\alpha_{1-t}'}{\sqrt{1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2}}
$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
u_t(x_t|x_1) &= \frac{\sigma_t'(x_1)}{\sigma_t(x_1)} (x_t - \mu_t(x_1)) + \mu_t'(x_1) \\
&= \frac{\alpha_{1-t}\alpha_{1-t}'}{\sqrt{1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2}} \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2}} (x_t - \alpha_{1-t}x_1) - \alpha_{1-t}'x_1 \\
&= \frac{\alpha_{1-t}\alpha_{1-t}'x_t - \alpha_{1-t}^2\alpha_{1-t}'x_1 - (1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2)\alpha_{1-t}'x_1}{1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2} \\
&= \frac{\alpha_{1-t}'(\alpha_{1-t}x_t - x_1)}{1 - \alpha_{1-t}^2}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 8.3 小测验2：简单线性路径

设：

$$
p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(tx_1, (1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})t)I)
$$

**问：**$u_t(x_t|x_1)$是什么？

**答案：**

$$
\mu_t(x_1) = tx_1 \Rightarrow \frac{d}{dt}\mu_t(x_1) = x_1
$$
$$
\sigma_t(x_1) = 1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})t \Rightarrow \frac{d}{dt}\sigma_t(x_1) = -(1 - \sigma_{\min})
$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
u_t(x_t|x_1) &= \frac{\sigma_t'(x_1)}{\sigma_t(x_1)}(x_t - \mu_t(x_1)) + \mu_t'(x_1) \\
&= \frac{-(1 - \sigma_{\min})}{1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})t}(x_t - tx_1) + x_1 \\
&= \frac{x_1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})x_t}{1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})t}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 8.4 总结：一个简单的条件概率路径

$$
p_{t|1}(x_t|x_1) = \mathcal{N}(t x_1, (1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})t)I)
$$
$$
\phi_t(x_t|x_1) = (1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})t)x_t + t x_1
$$
$$
u_t(x_t|x_1) = \frac{x_1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})x_t}{1 - (1 - \sigma_{\min})t}
$$

本讲介绍了 Flow Matching 的基本思想，从 Normalizing Flow 到 Continuous Normalizing Flow，再到 Conditional Flow Matching，通过条件概率路径和条件向量场的构造，解决了传统流模型中计算复杂度高的问题。后续课程将进一步探讨 Flow Matching 与扩散模型的关系及其在实际中的应用。
